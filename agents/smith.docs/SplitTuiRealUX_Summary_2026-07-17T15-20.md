# Split TUI Uplift Real-terminal UX Review

Actual Split compression was exercised in 80x22 and 120x30 pseudo-terminals.

At 80x22 the view clearly shows aggregate progress, a visible `S01-S05 of 8 (+3)` range, WAIT lifecycle rows, the mirrored RATE chart, paging help, and fixed encoder/partition/pool cards. At 120x30 it expands to nine visible sectors, longer progress bars, a wider chart, more logs, and taller controls while preserving alignment.

One-based sector identities and text lifecycle states work without color. Pause/Throttle remain discoverable, while the three startup-fixed settings do not look adjustable. The `I` chart vocabulary matches Stream mode.

Real-terminal UX: APPROVED.
